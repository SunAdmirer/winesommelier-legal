[DEVELOPER_NAME] ("we", "us", or "our") operates the WineSommelier mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

  • Anonymous users: We generate a unique device identifier (vendor ID) to create an anonymous account. No personal information is required.
  • Sign in with Apple: If you choose to sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple user ID, display name (if provided), and email address (which may be a Private Relay address if you choose to hide your email). We do not receive your Apple ID password.

1.2 Photos

When you use the scan feature, you take or select photos of wine labels, wine menus, or food. These images are:

  • Compressed to JPEG format (max ~5 MB) before upload
  • Sent to our servers over HTTPS for AI analysis
  • Stored on our servers to provide scan history and results

Photos are used solely for the purpose of generating wine recommendations and are not shared with third parties for marketing.

1.3 Taste Preferences

You may provide wine and food taste preferences (e.g., sweetness, body, preferred wine types, food categories). These are stored on our servers to personalize recommendations.

1.4 Scan History & Favorites

We store your scan history (wines you've scanned) and favorites (wines you've saved) on our servers, linked to your account.

1.5 Purchase Information

If you subscribe to WineSommelier Premium, Apple processes the payment. We receive a purchase receipt to validate your subscription status. We do not have access to your payment card details.

1.6 Device & Language Information

We collect your device's preferred language settings to deliver localized content (AI-generated notes, recommendations). We do not collect device model, OS version, or other hardware identifiers beyond the vendor ID.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Google AdMob

For free (non-premium) users, we display advertisements through Google AdMob. On first launch we ask two permissions, in this order:

  • EU / UK / Switzerland consent (UMP) — users in the European Economic Area, UK, and Switzerland see Google's consent form for GDPR compliance before any ad is loaded.
  • App Tracking Transparency (ATT) — a system prompt asking whether WineSommelier may use your device's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) to personalize ads. If you decline, ads still work but are non-personalized.

Google's handling of advertising data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy and AdMob data disclosure. Premium subscribers do not see ads, and no ad-related data (including IDFA) is collected for them.

3.2 Apple Services

We use Apple's Sign in with Apple and StoreKit frameworks. Apple's handling of your data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Authentication tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain (hardware-encrypted).
  • All network communication uses HTTPS encryption.
  • We do not store passwords — authentication is handled via Apple's identity system or anonymous device tokens.
  • Server-side data is stored in secure, access-controlled databases.

5. Data Retention

  • Your account data, scan history, favorites, and taste preferences are retained as long as your account exists.
  • When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Logging out invalidates your session but retains data for Apple-linked accounts (so you can sign back in).

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Delete your account: You can delete your account and all associated data from within the App (Profile → Delete Account).
  • Manage favorites & history: You can remove individual favorites at any time.
  • Ad personalization: You can revoke or change your tracking permission at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → WineSommelier. Disabling tracking switches ads to non-personalized mode; it does not remove ads entirely (upgrade to Premium to remove ads).
  • Sign in with Apple: You can stop using your Apple ID with the App via Settings → Apple ID → Sign-In & Security → Sign in with Apple.

7. Children's Privacy

WineSommelier is intended for users of legal drinking age in their jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child, we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Effective Date" at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
